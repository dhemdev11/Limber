# Limber — Capstone Deliverable Index
### The science of a more adaptable mind · Global · Neuroplasticity-core · June 2026
> A complete, evidence-backed product package: deep global neuroplasticity research → world-class strategy → full artefact suite → interactive prototype → rubric-scored review. Every claim is sourced and evidence-graded; the product is built to refuse the claims the science won't support.

## The one-line thesis
**Train the brain the way it actually changes — honestly.** A global, neuroplasticity-core daily practice (**Prime → Train → Consolidate**, adaptive ~85% challenge zone, lifestyle levers, personal-baseline progress, an evidence library) whose **moat is evidence-honesty** in a category defined by overclaiming (Lumosity's $2M FTC fine).

## Process
A global neuroplasticity research team (mechanisms, products, specialists, clinical efficacy, market, UX, personas) → synthesis into a neuroplasticity-core thesis → evidence-backed artefacts → an independent rubric examiner (gaps fixed, re-checked). Every claim is Strong/Moderate/Weak-graded; null results, retractions, and the FTC precedent are surfaced, not hidden.
