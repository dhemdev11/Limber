# Case Study — Limber
### From a problem statement to a global, neuroplasticity-core product · June 2026

## The brief (evolved)
Begin from a mental-wellness brief; then pivot to a **global** product with **neuroplasticity as the literal core**, built to world-class standards. The mandate: research neuroplasticity deeply worldwide and let the evidence — not hype — define the product.

## The challenge
Neuroplasticity is the most over-marketed idea in consumer health. The category's defining event is **Lumosity's $2M FTC fine** for overclaiming, and a 70-scientist consensus calling industry marketing "exaggerated." Meanwhile **far-transfer is null** (training rarely generalizes to real-world cognition), **~70%** of these apps are abandoned within 100 days, and the strongest "rewiring" claims (mindfulness restructuring the brain) failed a rigorous RCT and rest on a **retracted** meta-analysis. Building credibly here means *resisting* the very claims that sell.

## What we did
A global research team produced seven evidence-graded memos: mechanisms (2023–26), a teardown of every major neuroplasticity/brain-training/neurofeedback/DTx product, the specialist frameworks (anchored by **Kleim & Jones' 10 Principles**), the clinical efficacy & claims boundary, global market sizing, a world-class UX/UI design system, and global personas + adherence science. Every claim carries a Strong/Moderate/Weak grade, and null results and retractions are surfaced deliberately.

## The insight
The science gives a precise, ownable recipe — *attended, adaptive, spaced, meaningful practice* sitting on the **lifestyle levers that gate plasticity** (exercise/BDNF, sleep, stress, novelty). Yet **no product integrates that full stack honestly**: BrainHQ trains cognition (no feedback, no levers), Muse gives feedback (thin proof), wellness apps ride a halo, biohacking gadgets overclaim. The white space is the **honest, integrated middle**.

## The decision
**Limber — the science of a more adaptable mind.** Neuroplasticity at the core, operationalized as **Prime → Train → Consolidate** with adaptive difficulty held in the **~85% challenge zone**, the lifestyle levers as first-class inputs, progress shown against the user's **own baseline**, and an **evidence library** stating what we will and won't claim. **Honesty is the moat** — a differentiator competitors structurally can't copy without abandoning their claims. Global from day one (WCAG 2.2 AA, i18n, offline), beachhead the science-literate NA optimizer/longevity cohort who *reward* rigor, scale via employers and an EU **DiGA** track.

## The solution
An interactive prototype demonstrates the loop end-to-end with a **real adaptive training task** (live accuracy → auto step-up/down to hold the challenge zone), a live reaction-time **baseline**, a **personal-baseline progress radar**, the lifestyle levers, and the evidence library — all in a calm, accessible, global design system.

## The outcome (what this submission contains)
A complete, evidence-backed suite: neuroplasticity science core, global market & competitive dossier, strategy, TAM/SAM/SOM + BMC ($58B/$8.8B/~$103M), six global personas with empathy maps, IA + all-persona flows, an interactive prototype, BRD, GTM + funnel, a pitch deck, a transparent GenAI acknowledgment — each reviewed against the "Excellent" rubric.

## What we'd validate next
Retention and efficacy are the #1 risks and are named, not hidden: the immediate next step is a **design-partner pilot** proving the loop beats the ~70% abandonment rate and delivers measurable near-transfer — plus a published evidence program that makes the honesty real.
