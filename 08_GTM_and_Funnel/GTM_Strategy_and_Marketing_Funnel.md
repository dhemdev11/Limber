# GTM Strategy & Marketing Funnel — Limber (Global)
### Worldwide launch plan + AARRR funnel · June 2026
> Rebuilt for the global, neuroplasticity-core scope, grounded in the segment, adherence, and channel research.

## 1. Segmentation & beachhead
**Beachhead:** **North American optimizers/longevity enthusiasts** (25–55, science-literate, $30–100+/mo WTP, already in the Oura/Whoop/Huberman ecosystem). They pay, evangelize, and *reward evidence-honesty*. **Expansion:** (2) worried-well aging (largest, credibility-driven; + European **DiGA** track); (3) students globally (freemium scale); (4) clinical/recovery via partnerships/reimbursement; (5) longevity integrators.

## 2. Positioning & message
*"Train a more adaptable mind — the way the science actually says it changes."* Lead with **rigor and honesty**: personal-baseline progress, a public "what the evidence shows/doesn't" library, named advisors. The anti-hype brand in a hype-saturated category.

## 3. Channel strategy (ranked, evidence-based)
1. **Creator + community (Priority 1)** — science/longevity creators (Huberman/Attia-adjacent), podcasts, Reddit (r/Nootropics, r/Biohacking), longevity communities. Honesty earns credibility with this audience; lowest-CAC, highest-trust entry.
2. **Employer / B2B (Priority 2, scale engine)** — corporate cognitive-wellbeing (fastest-growing channel ~17% CAGR; 52% of wellness employers integrate cognitive training); sold via benefits brokers; PEPM economics fix consumer ARPU limits.
3. **Wearable ecosystem partnerships (Priority 3)** — Oura/Apple Health/Whoop integrations as distribution + differentiation for the optimizer/longevity cohort.
4. **Europe DiGA / clinical (Priority 4, credibility + reimbursement)** — a regulated track that doubles as a trust signal globally.
*Plus:* evidence-led PR (publish studies), ASO, app-store features.

## 4. Pricing & monetization (global)
Free (baseline + limited loop; growth engine) · **Plus $12.99/mo · $89/yr** · **Pro $19.99/mo** (coaching + deeper personalization) · optional **hardware/biofeedback attach** (~$120/yr net) · **B2B employer ~$4–8 PEPM** (scale engine) · **DiGA reimbursed** (~€227/90-day, EU). 7-day Plus trial gate; annual-skew push for LTV.

## 5. Marketing funnel (AARRR)
| Stage | Goal | Tactics | Key metric |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Acquisition** | Low-CAC, high-trust reach | Science creators; podcasts; communities; ASO; wearable partners | CPI, organic %, creator-attributed |
| **Activation** | Value before signup | 3-min baseline → instant personal insight; goal tether | % reaching first insight/session |
| **Retention** | Beat ~70% churn | Visible progress vs baseline; ethical streaks (forgiveness); implementation intentions; coaching; meaning-tether | **Day-30/90 retention (north-star input)** |
| **Referral** | Trusted word-of-mouth | "Share your progress"; creator codes; community challenges | K-factor |
| **Revenue** | Sustainable monetization | Plus/Pro; B2B PEPM; hardware attach; DiGA | ARR, B2B mix, LTV:CAC |

*Retention is the crux — the category dies here; the science-honest loop + coaching + meaning-tether are the growth strategy, not just product.*

## 6. Launch plan (18 months)
- **Q1–Q2 — Pilot (NA optimizers):** prove Day-30/90 retention + near-transfer; build evidence base; 5–8 creator partners.
- **Q3 — Global GA:** full i18n/accessibility; expand creator + community; launch wearable integrations.
- **Q4 — B2B:** employer pilots via brokers; launch PEPM + aggregate dashboards.
- **Q5–Q6 — Credibility + reach:** publish first efficacy results; begin EU **DiGA** submission; expand to aging + student segments with tailored offers.

## 7. Budget logic
Front-load **evidence + creator/community** (trust is the moat); B2B sales as ARPU multiplier; reserve for DiGA/clinical evidence generation. Avoid broad paid UA until retention is proven.
